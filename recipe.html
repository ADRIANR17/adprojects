Chicken Curry

Chicken Curry is a popular Caribbean dish enjoyed across many households, ranging from Jamaica to Guyana. A dish made with chicken in a rich, flavorful sauce typically made with a blend of spices

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp of salt
  • Curry Powder
  • 1 onion
  • Few cloves of garlic
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1 can coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt, then brown in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onions and garlic in the same pot until softened.
  3. Add curry powder and Scotch Bonnet pepper, stirring until fragrant
  4. Return the chicken to the pot, coating it well with the spices.
  5. Pour in coconut milk, bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  6. Cook for 20–30 minutes, until chicken is tender and curry thickens. Enjoy!
